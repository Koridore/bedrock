With Firefox, you can pick up where you left off when you switch from desktop computer to mobile phone to tablet.

Firefox lets you see your bookmarks, but also your saved passwords and logins, no matter which device you’re using.

Sign up for a Firefox account, and you’ll be able to see your saved information like search history, recently closed tabs, bookmarks and logins on any device while using your Firefox browser. All of your data is encrypted on our servers, so only you can access it — we don’t sell it to advertisers, because that would go against our data privacy promise.
