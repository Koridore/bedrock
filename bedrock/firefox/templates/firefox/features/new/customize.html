Browser themes let you choose the color scheme or background image of your Firefox toolbar. They change the color of menus and Firefox system pages.

Firefox comes with a default system theme and is preloaded with light, dark and other colorful themes.

Browse the top-rated, trending and most recommended themes at addons.mozilla.org. Or look for themes by category, including music, seasonal, sports, and nature.
